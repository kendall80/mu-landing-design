Our Vision

Life is easier if you are in control of your finances. To be in control of your finances you have to know where your money is going and make sure it goes as far as it can. To achieve this we are building MU Money. We believe that the world of payments is changing and that we are on the brink of a revolution that can make peoples lives easier

Peoples lives became easier when we moved from bartering to using stone and metal money and when we moved from those to paper money and eventually to paying with plastic. Those changes brought convenience and made sure payment became standardized across different location

The new revolution in mobile payment is less about convenience and reach, and more about information and understanding. Knowing how you spent your money, knowing where to get the most in return for your money and getting things that you want in return for sharing that information

From credit to loyalty cards, big companies collect data about you. Just like Google and Facebook do. Google and Facebook let you see your data and give you a service in return. We feel it should be the same with payment. If you are going to give up data you may as well know what it is and get something you like in return
