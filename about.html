The Vision

Life is easier if you are in control of your finances. To be in control of your finances you have to know where your money is going and help it go as far as it can.

To achieve this Gabriele Grecchi and Sergej Tolz are building MU Money, which is a new ecosystem for payment consisting of MU Card (a debit, credit or prepaid card) and the MU Money portal (an internet portal and app), which will be your guide through finance and shopping.

Imagine having a lighter wallet. Not because you have less money, but because you get rid of all the junk in there. In exchange you get more spending power. Donâ€™t bother carrying around cash, credit and debit cards, loyalty scheme cards and vouchers as well as receipts. All you need is your MU Card and your MU Money on your smartphone.

MU Card will work like a normal payment card that you use at any merchant. However, there are some added bonuses. Your transactions will be recorded an categories, allowing you to see you spending patterns in the MU Money portal. You will be able to link your loyalty cards from your favourite merchants, which means you only need to swipe you MU Card to collect all the points and not have to pull out a second card. You can get set savings goals and the MU Money portal will help you achieve those by analysing your spending patterns with you.

Finally, MU Money makes your money go further by getting your discounts for items that you like by getting rewarded for referring friends to your favourite shops.
